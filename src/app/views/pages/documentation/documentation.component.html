Documentation

  1. You can update the company name and logo for use in reports.
  2. Before creating an employee, you must first create departments, designations, and job types.
  3. To mark attendance, the employee must be created first.
  4. You can create employee attendance records only once; to modify them later, use the edit option.
  5. The salary wizard operates based on attendance records.
  6. Deleted records cannot be recovered.
  7. If an employee is marked as inactive or deleted, all related records (attendance, leave applications, company assets, asset returns, and loans) will also be marked inactive or deleted accordingly.
  8. Loans, company assets, asset returns, leave applications, and attendance records can only be created for active employees.
  9. Images for users, employees, leave applications, and companies can be uploaded up to 5MB in PNG, JPG, JPEG, or WEBP format.
  10. You can generate reports only from the "Reports" menu.
  11. Reports can be downloaded in PDF and Excel formats.
  12. If you need assistance, feel free to contact our support team by clicking on Support.
