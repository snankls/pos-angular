Detailed Documentation

Dashboard Overview: Your central hub for business insights and quick access to key metrics.

  • Real-time Analytics: View daily sales performance, inventory status, and customer activity at a glance
  • Quick Actions: Access frequently used features directly from the dashboard
  • Performance Metrics: Monitor key business indicators to make informed decisions

Invoices
  • Create and manage sales transactions with detailed invoice generation
  • Add multiple products, apply discounts, and calculate taxes automatically
  • Print or email invoices directly to customers
  • Track invoice status (paid, unpaid, partially paid)
Returns
  • Process product returns and issue refunds efficiently
  • Automatically update inventory levels when returns are processed
  • Generate return receipts for customer records
  • Track return reasons and patterns for business analysis
Customers
  • Maintain comprehensive customer database with contact information
  • Track customer purchase history and preferences
  • Segment customers for targeted marketing and service
  • Manage customer credit limits and payment terms
Customer Payments
  • Record customer payments against outstanding invoices
  • Handle partial payments and payment plans
  • Track payment methods (cash, card, bank transfer, etc.)
  • Generate payment receipts and update customer balances automatically

Products Management
  • Product Listing: View and manage all products in your inventory
  • Add New Products: Create new product entries with detailed information including SKU, pricing, and descriptions
  • Brands Management: Organize products by brands for better categorization
  • Categories Management: Create product categories and subcategories for easy navigation
  • Units Management: Define measurement units (pieces, kg, liters, boxes, etc.) for products
Stocks Management
  • Real-time inventory tracking with automatic updates on sales and returns
  • Set low stock alerts to prevent inventory shortages
  • Track stock movement history and valuation
  • Manage multiple warehouse locations if applicable

Company Setup
  • Configure your business information (name, address, contact details)
  • Upload company logo for professional invoices and reports
  • Set up tax information and business registration details
  • Configure default currency and other business preferences
Bank Accounts
  • Manage multiple bank accounts for different payment methods
  • Track bank transactions and reconciliations
  • Set up account details for electronic payments
Cities/Locations
  • Manage business locations and branches
  • Set up delivery areas and service locations
  • Configure location-specific pricing and taxes
Import / Export
  • Bulk import products using CSV or Excel templates
  • Export sales data, inventory reports, and customer information
  • Backup and restore business data for security
  • Migrate data between different system instances

Customer Ledgers
  • Generate detailed customer transaction histories
  • View outstanding balances and payment due dates
  • Track customer credit utilization and payment patterns
  • Export ledger reports for accounting purposes
Product Stocks Reports
  • View current stock levels across all products
  • Generate stock valuation reports
  • Identify slow-moving and fast-moving items
  • Track inventory turnover rates
  • Monitor stock aging and expiration dates

Global Configuration
  • Configure system preferences and default settings
  • Set up tax rates and calculation methods
  • Manage invoice numbering formats and sequences
  • Configure receipt printing settings
  • Set up user permissions and access controls
  • Manage backup schedules and data retention policies
Important Notes & Best Practices
  • Setup Order: Configure Company settings first, then set up Products with Categories and Brands before processing sales
  • Data Integrity: Always use the Returns feature instead of deleting invoices to maintain accurate financial records
  • Inventory Accuracy: Regular stock counts help maintain accurate inventory levels in the system
  • Customer Management: Add customers before creating invoices to track customer history and outstanding balances
  • Backup Regularly: Use the Export feature to regularly backup your business data
  • Report Generation: All business reports are available in the Reports section with PDF and Excel export options
  • Support: For technical assistance, visit our Support page
Recommended Workflow
  1. Start with Administration → Company setup
  2. Configure Inventory → Products (Categories, Brands, Units)
  3. Add your Customers database
  4. Process daily Sales through Invoices
  5. Record Customer Payments as received
  6. Monitor Stocks and generate Reports regularly
  7. Use System Settings to customize according to your business needs
